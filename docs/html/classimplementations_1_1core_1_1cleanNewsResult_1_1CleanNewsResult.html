Receives a trends and an article. If the trend already exists in the trendListMap it just gets the list, adds the article and puts it back in the map If the trend was not previously registered it creates a new list, adds the article and puts the relation of the trend and the list in the map.

Parameters
trendThe trend to which the article you are passing is related to
articleThe article you want to save
